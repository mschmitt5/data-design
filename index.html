User Story

User Tiberius reads an article by Dr. McCoy and loves it so much he would like to follow the author so he won't miss any of his posts.

Use Case and Interaction Flow

  1. 1. Tiberius clicks an article to read.
  2. 2. The site shows him the article and he loves it!
  3. 3.Tiberius clicks "clap" many times to show his appreciation.
  4. He also clicks "Follow" on the author's mini profile at the bottom of the page.
  5. He will now be notified of any more interesting articles by Dr. McCoy.
